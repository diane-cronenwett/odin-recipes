Macaroni Recipe

Home

Description

This macaroni recipe is a classic comfort food that is easy to make and delicious. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy family meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the milk and cheese, stirring until the cheese is melted and the mixture is smooth.
  4. Combine the cooked macaroni with the cheese sauce. Mix well.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve warm.
